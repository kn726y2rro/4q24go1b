How to Choose the Best Longboard Wheels


Longboarding is a popular activity that involves riding a longboard on a skateboard. One of the most important parts of a longboard is the wheels. Choosing the right wheels can make a huge difference in your longboarding experience. In this article, we will discuss how to choose the best longboard wheels.


Factors to Consider


When choosing longboard wheels, there are several factors to consider. The most important factors are the size, hardness, and material of the wheels.


Size


The size of the wheels is determined by the length of the skateboard. The longer the skateboard, the larger the wheels should be. Generally, longboards come in two sizes: standard and wide. Standard longboards have wheels that are around 78mm in diameter, while wide longboards have wheels that are around 82mm in diameter.


Hardness


The hardness of the wheels determines how fast and smooth they roll. The most common types of wheels are urethane and polyurethane. Urethane wheels are softer and faster, while polyurethane wheels are harder and slower.


Material


The material of the wheels is also important. Wheels made of wood or bamboo are heavier and slower, while wheels made of plastic or composite are lighter and faster.


Conclusion


In conclusion, choosing the best longboard wheels involves considering the size, hardness, and material of the wheels. By considering these factors, you can choose the right wheels for your longboard and improve your longboarding experience.
