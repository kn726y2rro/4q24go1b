What Are Nootropics?


Nootropics are a type of supplement that can help improve brain function and concentration. They work by boosting the production of neurotransmitters, such as dopamine and serotonin, which are responsible for regulating mood, memory, and concentration. Nootropics are also known as "smart drugs" or "cognitive enhancers."


How Do Nootropics Work?


Nootropics work by increasing the production of neurotransmitters in the brain. They do this by boosting the activity of certain enzymes, such as monoamine oxidase (MAO) and acetylcholinesterase (AChE). By increasing the activity of these enzymes, nootropics can help improve brain function and concentration.

Side Effects of Nootropics


While nootropics can have many benefits, they can also have some potential side effects. Here are some common side effects of nootropics:
