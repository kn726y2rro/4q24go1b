How to Choose the Best Lash Adhesive


Lash adhesives are essential for anyone who wants to wear false eyelashes. Without the right adhesive, your lashes will not stay in place all day, and you may end up with an unpleasant experience. Choosing the best lash adhesive can be a challenge, especially with so many options available in the market. Here are some tips to help you select the best lash adhesive for your needs.


What to Look for in a Lash Adhesive?


Before purchasing a lash adhesive, here are some things to consider:


Waterproof


Most lash adhesives are waterproof, but some are water-resistant. If you plan to wear your lashes in the pool or shower, it's essential to choose a waterproof adhesive.


Hold Time


The hold time is the amount of time the adhesive will keep your lashes in place. Most lash adhesives have a hold time of 8-12 hours, but some can last up to 24 hours.


Drying Time


Drying time is the amount of time it takes for the glue to become tacky and ready to be applied to your lashes. Most lash adhesives take 5-10 minutes to dry, but some can take as long as 30 minutes.


Smell and Toxins


Some lash adhesives have a strong smell, which can be unpleasant. Also, avoid lash adhesives that contain toxins or harmful chemicals.


Types of Lash Adhesives


There are two types of lash adhesives:


Dual-side Adhesive


This type of lash adhesive has a sticky side that is applied to your eyelashes, and a separate adhesive that is applied to the false eyelashes. This type of adhesive is easy to apply and remove.


Single-side Adhesive


This type of lash adhesive has a single sided adhesive that is applied to your eyelashes and false eyelashes. This type of adhesive is more difficult to apply, but lasts longer.


Our Recommendation


After considering the above factors, we recommend using the Revlon Lash Adhesive. This lash adhesive is waterproof and has a long hold time of up to 24 hours. It dries quickly and has a pleasant scent. It contains toxins and is easy to apply.


We hope this guide helps you choose the best lash adhesive for your needs. Remember to choose a waterproof adhesive with a long hold time and a pleasant scent.
