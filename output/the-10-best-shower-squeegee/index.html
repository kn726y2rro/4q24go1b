How to Choose the Best Shower Squeegee


Shower squeegees are an essential tool for keeping your bathroom clean and tidy. They are particularly useful for removing water from the shower after use, which can help prevent mold and mildew buildup. However, with so many different types of squeegees on the market, it can be difficult to know which one to choose. In this article, we will guide you through the process of selecting the best shower squeegee for your needs.


Types of Shower Squeegees


There are two main types of shower squeegees: plastic and stainless steel. Plastic shower squeegees are cheaper and more affordable, but they may not be as durable as stainless steel squeegees. Stainless steel squeegees are more durable and long-lasting, but they can be more expensive.


Considerations


When choosing a shower squeegee, there are a few key considerations to keep in mind. Firstly, you need to consider the size of your bathtub or shower. A large shower may require a larger squeegee to reach all corners, while a smaller shower may require a smaller squeegee. Secondly, you need to consider the material of the squeegee. If you have a shower with a glass door, you may want to choose a squeegee with a soft, rubber blade to prevent scratching the glass. Finally, you need to consider the handle of the squeegee. Some squeegees have a fixed handle that cannot be adjusted, while others have a telescoping handle that can be extended or shortened to reach different heights.


Choosing a Shower Squeegee


Now that you have an idea of the different types of shower squeegees and considerations to keep in mind, it's time to choose the best one for your needs. Here are some tips for selecting the best shower squeegee:


- Choose a squeegee with a soft, rubber blade to prevent scratching the glass in your shower.


- Consider the size of your bathtub or shower. A larger shower may require a larger squeegee.


- Look for a squeegee with a durable, long-lasting handle.


- Read customer reviews to get an idea of the quality of the squeegee.


By following these tips, you can choose the best shower squeegee for your needs and keep your bathroom clean and tidy.
