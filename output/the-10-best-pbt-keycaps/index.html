How to Choose the Best PBT Keycaps


PBT (polybutylene terephthalate) keycaps are becoming increasingly popular among gamers and typists alike. They offer a softer, smoother feel than traditional ABS keycaps, and they are more durable and resistant to wear and tear. If you're in the market for PBT keycaps, it can be overwhelming trying to choose the best ones for your needs. Here are some things to consider when selecting PBT keycaps:


Material


The first thing to consider when choosing PBT keycaps is the material they are made from. PBT is a type of plastic that is durable, resistant to wear and tear, and offers a soft, smooth feel. Some keycaps are made from a mixture of PBT and ABS, which can offer a different feel and durability than all-PBT keycaps.


Size


Another important consideration is the size of the keycaps. There are two main sizes of PBT keycaps: single-color and double-color. Single-color keycaps have a single color on each side, while double-color keycaps have a color on one side and a clear, transparent material on the other.


Texture


The texture of the keycaps is also an important consideration. Some PBT keycaps have a smooth, matte texture, while others have a glossy, metallic texture. Some people prefer the smooth, matte texture, while others prefer the glossy, metallic texture.


Design


Finally, consider the design of the keycaps. There are many different designs and colors to choose from, so you can find keycaps that match your personality and gaming style. Some keycaps have unique designs that offer a more personalized touch to your keyboard setup.


By considering these factors, you can choose the best PBT keycaps for your needs. Whether you're a gamer, a typist, or both, PBT keycaps offer a soft, smooth feel that is sure to improve your typing experience.
